About Us

For more than 25 years, Community News has been providing services to our local community leagues. We publish a network of community league newspapers that reaches over 100,000 households and 50 communities.

Through our nine newspapers, communities keep residents up-to-date about programs, social events and local issues. Community league editors welcome submissions about local happenings.

If you have questions about the papers, please contact your newsletter editor.
