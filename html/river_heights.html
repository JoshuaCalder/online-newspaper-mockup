About River Heights

River Heights is a free community newsletter that is delivered by Canada Post to over 22,000 households. Published 10 times a year, it proudly serves the following 5 community leagues:

  • Dunwood
  • Mordor
  • Lucerno
  • Mohonri
  • Oakwood

To read the latest issue of River Heights, click the "Read Now" button. If you would like to read a previous issue, select an issue from the archives.
